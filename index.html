How to export from Instagram

  1. Instagram → Settings & privacy → Your activity → Download your information
  2. Select account → Some of your information → check Followers and following
  3. Choose Download to device → format JSON → Create files
  4. Instagram emails you a zip — unzip, then upload followers_1.json and following.json below
