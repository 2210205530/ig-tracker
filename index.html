How to export from Instagram

  1. Open Instagram → tap your profile → top-right menu (☰) → Settings & privacy
  2. Scroll to Your activity → Download your information → Download or transfer information
  3. Select your account → Some of your information → check Followers and following
  4. Choose Download to device → select JSON format → tap Create files
  5. Instagram emails you a link — download the zip, unzip it, then upload followers_1.json and following.json below
