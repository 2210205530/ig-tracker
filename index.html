  1. Instagram → profile → menu (☰) → Settings & privacy
  2. Your activity → Download your information → Download or transfer
  3. Pick your account → Some of your information → tick Followers and following
  4. Download to device → format JSON → Create files
  5. Unzip the email link, then drop followers_1.json and following.json below
