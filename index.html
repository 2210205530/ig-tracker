How to export from Instagram
  1. Open Instagram → Settings → Your activity
  2. Tap Download your information → Download or transfer information
  3. Select Followers and following, choose JSON format
  4. Request download — Instagram will email you a zip file
  5. Unzip and upload followers_1.json and following.json below
